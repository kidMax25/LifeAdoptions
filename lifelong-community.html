LifeLong Adoptions supports the following charities:

  • March of Dimes fights for the health of all moms and babies so every baby can have the best possible start.
  • Newborns in Need makes sure moms and families are supported in the first moments of the child’s life.
  • Cradles to Crayons provides children from birth through age 12, living in homeless or low-income situations, with the essential items they need to thrive – at home, at school and at play.
  • National Safe Haven Alliance supports states’ efforts to prevent infanticide and newborn abandonment through safe-haven relinquishments.

Share your adoption story

If you adopted with us, you truly are a part of our ever-growing adoption family. Only those touched by adoption can truly understand the dynamics of adoption and how greatly it alters your emotions, your priorities, your family, and your life. By sharing your story, you will enlighten and encourage others and give hope to those thinking about starting their own adoption journey.

Home of the Sparrow Gala

LifeLong team members attend the Home of the Sparrow 35th Anniversary Fundraising Gala. These life-changing funds will assist women and children with case management services, basic needs care, housing and utility payments, career and job assistance through the Employment Program, counseling, and more! Learn more about Home of the Sparrow’s mission to reduce homelessness among women and children at www.hosparrow.org

Volunteer Yardwork

LifeLong staff doing yardwork and lawn clean-up at a local transitional home for women and children operated by Home of the Sparrow. The organization works to reduce homelessness among women and children. To learn more about their mission, visit www.hosparrow.org

Cradles to Crayons Donation

The LifeLong team celebrates another year of adoptions by making a donation to Cradles to Crayons. The organization works to help the more than 20 million children in the United States who do not have the clothing and basic essentials they need for healthy childhood development. To learn more, visit www.cradlestocrayons.org

Cradles to Crayons Donation

The LifeLong team held a collection for donations to the organization Cradles to Crayons. The organization helps provide children in need with basic essentials they may not have access to, like clothing, shoes, diapers, and school supplies. Learn more about their mission at www.cradlestocrayons.org.

LifeLong Family Reunion

The LifeLong team held a reunion event in Dallas, Texas, for LifeLong families to come together and celebrate adoption. Families flew in from all across the country, and the team loved getting to meet these families in person. Learn more about our reunion event by clicking here.

Chicago Pride Fest

The LifeLong team participated in the Chicago Pride Fest and had a blast spreading the word among attendees about LGBTQ+ adoption. Chicago Pride Fest is an annual street festival where thousands gather to celebrate LGBTQ+ life, culture, and community. Learn more at www.northalsted.com/pridefest

As a traditional and LGBT adoption service provider, LifeLong Adoptions believes in the power and significance of family and that one can be formed in many different ways. Our philosophy is based on one-on-one guidance, providing adoptive parents with an adoption specialist to guide you through the process.
