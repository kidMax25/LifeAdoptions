Hi, we're LifeLong Adoptions. Since 2011 we have helped hundreds of families achieve their dreams of parenthood through the miracle of infant adoption. Free Application »

We’d be honored to be part of your domestic adoption story.

LifeLong Adoptions is dedicated to growing families through adoption by preparing, nurturing, supporting, and advocating for all those involved in the infant adoption process. Our caring and compassionate team will guide you through every step of your newborn adoption journey and our expert marketing and advertising services will help you reach as many potential birthmothers as possible.

Expectant mothers interested in adoption for their baby can reach out to us for caring and non-judgmental assistance whenever and however they are most comfortable. If you're pregnant and wish to learn more about placing your baby for adoption, contact us to learn about your adoption options and get free information right away. Our team of birthmother coordinators is available 24/7 to answer calls, text messages, live chats, and emails from current and potential birthmothers.

Safe & Secure

At LifeLong Adoptions, we prioritize your peace of mind throughout the adoption process. Here's how we ensure a safe and secure adoption experience:

  • Legal Compliance & Ethical Standards: Rest assured, we operate lawfully under Illinois state law, upholding the highest moral and ethical standards in every aspect of our services.

  • Not an Agency or Facilitator: LifeLong Adoptions is not an adoption agency or facilitator, and we do not engage in adoption matching. We focus on providing a transparent and ethical platform for your adoption journey.

  • Supervised by Lutheran Child & Family Services (LCFS): We operate under the official supervision and oversight of LCFS, a licensed child-placing adoption agency. All payments for our services are made directly to LCFS, never to LifeLong Adoptions. This collaboration ensures legal compliance and an additional layer of security for our clients.

The Newborn Baby Adoption Process

Adopting a baby is not something you can do on your own. You need the knowledge and experience of adoption professionals like us behind you. Our dedicated team will help you throughout the entire adoption process and answer any questions you have along the way. Our approach is guided by our compassion for adopting families and respect for birthmothers.
